## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn for a telecom company (**Databel**)

Customer churn is a key business problem for subscription-based companies. This project focuses not only on measuring churn rate but also on identifying **why customers leave** and providing **data-driven recommendations** to reduce churn.

---

## Objectives
- Calculate overall churn rate  
- Identify key churn drivers  
- Analyze customer demographics and behavior  
- Build interactive dashboards  
- Provide actionable recommendations  

---

## Key Metrics
- **Average Churn Rate:** 26.86%  
- **California Churn Rate:** 63.24%  
- **Senior Citizen Churn Rate:** 38.46%  

---

## Key Findings

### Overall Churn
- Churn rate is **26.86%**, which is relatively high  

---

### Competitor Impact
- **Almost 50% of churn is related to competitors**  
- Indicates weak competitive positioning  

---

### Geography
- **California churn rate: 63.24% (extremely high)**  
- Suggests regional issues  

---

### Demographics (Age)
- Churn increases with age  
- **Senior citizens churn rate: 38.46%**  
- Age group **70+ has highest churn** but small population  

---

### Contracts
- **Month-to-month contracts → highest churn**  
- **1-year / 2-year contracts → much lower churn**  
- Two-year contracts have extremely low churn (~2–3%)  

---

### Group Contracts
- Group customers:
  - **Churn rate: 6.51% (very low)**  
  - Lower monthly charges  

- However:
  - **75%+ customers are NOT in a group**  

- **Group size 6 has the lowest churn rate**  

---

### Data Usage Behavior
- Customers with **unlimited plan but <5GB usage churn more**  
- Indicates overpaying → dissatisfaction  

---

### International Plan Insights
- Customers with:
  - International plan but no usage → **very high churn**  
  - Highest monthly charges  

- In California:
  - **72% churn among customers calling internationally without a plan**  

---

### Payment Behavior
- Paper check users:
  - Only ~5.5% of customers  
  - Pattern unclear due to small sample size  

---

### Extra Charges
- Customers without unlimited plan using ≥10GB:
  - **Average extra charges: 31.19**  

---

### Missing Data
- Some customers have **null churn reasons**  
- Indicates lack of feedback collection  

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Improve Competitive Strategy
- Re-evaluate pricing and services  
- Introduce retention offers  

---

### 2. Promote Long-Term Contracts
- Encourage 1-year and 2-year contracts  
- Offer discounts and incentives  

---

### 3. Expand Group Plans
- Promote family/group plans  
- Target individual customers  

---

### 4. Optimize Data Plans
- Identify low-usage unlimited customers  
- Recommend cheaper plans  

---

### 5. Fix International Plan Issues
- Suggest downgrades for unused plans  
- Promote plans to active international callers  

---

### 6. Target High-Risk Segments
- Senior citizens  
- Month-to-month users  
- High-bill customers  

---

### 7. Regional Strategy (California)
- Investigate competition and pricing  
- Launch targeted retention campaigns  

---
